> **Seed page demonstrating the concept format. Delete once real ingestion begins.**

Durable carbon dioxide removal (CDR) refers to approaches that remove CO₂ from the atmosphere
and store it for long (often century-to-millennial) timescales. Used here to demonstrate how a
concept page links to the sources and entities that reference it.
