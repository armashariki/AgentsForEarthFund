# raw/ — source documents

Drop the documents you want to ingest into this folder (`befkb ingest raw/<file>.pdf`).

**PDF binaries are intentionally not committed** to the repository (`raw/*.pdf` is gitignored) — they are
often copyrighted and bloat the repo. The two open‑access papers used in the worked examples
([../docs/USE_CASES.md](../docs/USE_CASES.md)) are listed below so you can fetch them yourself:

| File (drop here) | Paper | DOI |
|---|---|---|
| `Earth s Future - 2026 - Alejo ... .pdf` | Alejo et al. (2026), *Maximizing Nature‑Based Solutions Using Artificial Intelligence to Align Global Biodiversity, Climate, and Water Targets*, **Earth's Future** | [10.1029/2025EF007560](https://doi.org/10.1029/2025EF007560) |
| `s44168-026-00376-0.pdf` | Stein (2026), *AI for climate: from technological solutions to relational accountability*, **npj Climate Action** | [10.1038/s44168-026-00376-0](https://doi.org/10.1038/s44168-026-00376-0) |

Both are Creative Commons (CC BY‑NC‑ND) open‑access articles; download the PDFs from the DOI links above
and place them here to reproduce the examples. Any of your own PDFs work just as well.
